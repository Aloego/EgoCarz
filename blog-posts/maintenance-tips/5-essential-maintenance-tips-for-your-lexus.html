5 Essential Maintenance Tips for Your Lexus

3 min read  |  Maintenance Tips

Keep your Lexus running smoothly and reliably for years to come with these expert maintenance tips. Whether you drive an IS, RX, or ES, these steps will help you protect your investment and enjoy a worry-free driving experience.

1. Regular Oil Changes

Engine oil is the lifeblood of your Lexus. Change your oil and oil filter every 5,000 to 10,000 km (or as recommended in your owner’s manual) to ensure optimal engine performance and longevity. Use only high-quality oil suitable for your model.

2. Inspect and Replace Air Filters

Clean air filters improve fuel efficiency and engine health. Check your engine and cabin air filters every 10,000 km and replace them if they appear dirty or clogged. This is especially important in dusty environments.

3. Monitor Tire Pressure and Condition

Properly inflated tires improve safety, fuel economy, and ride comfort. Check your tire pressure monthly and before long trips. Rotate your tires every 10,000 km and inspect for uneven wear or damage.

4. Keep Up with Fluid Levels

Regularly check coolant, brake fluid, transmission fluid, and windshield washer fluid. Top up or replace as needed, and watch for leaks under your vehicle. Low or dirty fluids can lead to costly repairs.

5. Schedule Professional Inspections

Even if your Lexus seems fine, have it inspected by a certified technician at least once a year. They can spot issues early, update software, and ensure your car’s systems are running perfectly.
